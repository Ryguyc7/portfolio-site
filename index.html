About

I’m Ryan, a developer/designer who takes ideas from concept to launch. I’ve shipped iOS games with Unity + C# and built full-stack web apps using Firebase + Node.js.

I work across both front end and back end: React, JS/HTML/CSS for clean, responsive UIs, and Firestore, Auth, Hosting, Cloud Functions for scalable backend systems. I also integrate payments with Stripe.

I focus on speed, usability, and smooth motion. Whether it’s a game, a SaaS tool, or a realtime web app, I build products that feel good to use and are easy to maintain.
